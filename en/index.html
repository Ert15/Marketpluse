About the Product

Our e-commerce analytics tool is based on data parsing and is already successfully used for consumer electronics (mainly LG and Samsung) on the Kaspi.kz platform. It helps track the market, analyze prices, product positions, seller ratings, and trends.

What’s Included?

  • Price Monitoring:Real-time competitor price analysis.
  • Seller Ratings:Review overview, price dynamics, and search rankings.
  • Discount Tracking:Identify who and when prices are reduced.
  • Historical Data:Historical analysis of prices, ratings, and reviews.

Who is it for?

  • For Sellers:Market transparency, competitor monitoring, and price strategy optimization.
  • For Brands:Control product positions and assess partner performance.
  • For Marketers:Data for campaign planning and demand forecasting.

Upcoming Improvements

We plan to integrate with Halyk Market, Ozon, Wildberries, and other platforms and expand functionality to all categories of consumer electronics. Currently, the database exceeds 20 million rows, requiring additional computing power.
