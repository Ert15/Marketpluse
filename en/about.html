About the Product

Our analytics tool for e-commerce is built on data parsing and is already successfully running for consumer electronics (mainly LG and Samsung) on Kaspi.kz. It helps monitor the market, track prices, product positions, seller ratings, and identify trends.

What’s Included?

  • Price Monitoring: Real-time competitor price analysis.
  • Seller Ratings: Reviews, price dynamics, and search ranking.
  • Discount Tracking: Identify who lowers prices and when.
  • Historical Data: Retrospective analysis of prices, ratings, and reviews.

Who Benefits?

  • Sellers: Gain market transparency, monitor competitors, and optimize pricing strategies.
  • Brands: Track product positions and evaluate partner performance.
  • Marketers: Obtain data for promotional strategies and demand forecasting.

Future Enhancements

We plan to integrate with Halyk Market, Ozon, Wildberries, and other marketplaces, as well as expand functionality to cover all consumer electronics. Currently, the dataset is around 20 million rows, requiring additional processing power for optimal Power BI performance.
